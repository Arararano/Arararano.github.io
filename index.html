About Me

Hey!! I'm Arno, a student pursuing my BS in CS.

I love playing games and reading books. I also love linux, programming, math and other related fields. I upload myself programming every day on my youtube channel.

I've been using Arch for about 2 years now and recently switched my desktop over from windows aswell. I was drawn in by the creativity and expressiveness of the operating system, and I loved the look of i3. Growing up I used to write scripts for Cheat Engine to give me and my sisters infinite gems in Cooking Fever. I've been programming for 3 years now, primarily in Python, Java, and Rust, although recently I've taken an interest in learning C and Go.

I love building systems or software that I can use in my daily life, and I often find other people who build these types of things out of passion very inspiring. I really enjoy the youtuber JVscholz and his focus on being intentional about how you spend your time.

This past year I've adopted the habit of reading daily, if you have any book reccomendations feel free to reach out and let me know! I have recently enjoyed reading productivity books as well as books with related themes, and I've also recently finished book 4 of the Stormlight Archive by Brandon Sanderson.
